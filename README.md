# Pressure-Aware Auto Shop Reviews
## Problem
Consumers often feel pressured into unnecessary auto repairs through urgency framing and technical overwhelm. Existing review platforms focus on star ratings, not sales behavior transparency.

## Core Idea
A structured review platform that measures:
- 👩‍🔧 Explained in plain language
- 🔎 Evidence shown before recommendation
- 🚨 Use of urgency language
- 🙅‍♀️ Respect for declined services
- 🧾 Transparency of pricing
- 💬 Commentary about prior mechanics
- ✅ Customer confidence score

### Target Audience
- Non-technical car owners
- Young adults
- People new to cities

## Why It Matters
Auto repair is a high-trust, high-uncertainty industry. Non-technical consumers lack tools to evaluate sales pressure dynamics.
